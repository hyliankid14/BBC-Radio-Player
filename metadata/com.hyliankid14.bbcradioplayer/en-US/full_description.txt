British Radio Player is an unofficial third-party Android app for listening to BBC live radio and podcasts. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by the BBC.

Key features:
- Live BBC station streaming
- Podcast search, subscribe, and playback
- Episode progress and resume support
- Offline playback for downloaded episodes
- Media browser service and playback controls
- Background indexing and refresh workers

This app is independent and not affiliated with or endorsed by the BBC.
